Purpose of Tree Survival

Trees have a major contribution to the existence of human society and are important for our survival. Trees provide a natural ecological balance which helps to reduce floods and droughts and prevent soil erosion. The loss of trees and other vegetation can cause climate change, desertification, soil erosion, fewer crops, flooding, increased greenhouse gases in the atmosphere, and a host of problems for indigenous people.
